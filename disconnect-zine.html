DISCONNECT

A visual exploration of digital addiction • 2025

Concept

In a world flooded with glowing screens, I wanted to capture the feeling of being trapped inside one. Disconnect explores digital addiction through the lens of nostalgia and distortion.

Inspiration & Research

CRT screens, VHS tapes, retro monitors. Themes of time loss, endless scrolling, distorted identity.

Visual System

Typefaces: arcadeclassic, merchant-copy

Colors: RGB neon + grayscale base

Textures: Scanlines, VHS static, chromatic aberration overlays

Format: 1:1 ratio, 11 pages

Process & Iterations

Distorted photos into pixelated / low-res forms. Experimented with different levels of noise + scanline density.

Outcome

Both nostalgic and critical → scroll-stopping when presented digitally or printed.
