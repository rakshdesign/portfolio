Package Redesign

2025 · Packaging Design
A redesign of DOMS watercolour tube packaging, focusing on bold typography, modern minimalism, and clearer information hierarchy.

Reflections

I redesigned this packaging purely as a fun exercise studying the elements.
I wasn't solving a client brief, but to experiment with bold type, simplified layouts, and a more modern look.
The focus was to explore, testing how minimal typography and strong contrasts could change the feel of an everyday product.
